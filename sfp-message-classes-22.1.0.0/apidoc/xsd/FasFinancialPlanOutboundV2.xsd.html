  22     <xs:annotation>
  23         <xs:documentation>
  24             These messages pertain to student financial plan data:
  25             FasFinancialPlanOutboundV2Event: Students financial plan information, generated by internal events such as
  26             packaging/ repackaging.
  27             FasFinancialPlanOutboundV2Reply: Students financial plan information, generated by a customer request.
  28             FasFinancialPlanOutboundV2Request: The customer request for students financial plan information.
  29             FasFinancialPlanOutboundV2Event and FasFinancialPlanOutboundV2Response contain the same data. The only
  30             difference between the two messages is what triggers them.
  31         </xs:documentation>
  32     </xs:annotation>
