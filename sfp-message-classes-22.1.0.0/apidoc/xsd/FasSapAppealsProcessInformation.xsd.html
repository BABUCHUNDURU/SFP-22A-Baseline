  23         <xs:documentation>
  24             These messages are related to SAP appeals process information:
  25             FasSapAppealsProcessInformationEvent: SAP appeals process information, generated by internal events such as repackaging.
  26             FasSapAppealsProcessInformationReply: SAP appeals process information, generated by a customer request.
  27             FasSapAppealsProcessInformationRequest: The customer request.
  28             FasSapAppealsProcessInformationEventType and FasSapAppealsProcessInformationReply contain the same data.  The only
  29             difference between the two messages lies in what triggers them.
  30         </xs:documentation>
  31     </xs:annotation>
