Student Financial Planning : Disbursement

Created by Stephen Hutchinson, last modified by Angshuman Mukherjee on Feb 22, 2021

The Disbursement Subject contains Student Disbursement Information. The DISBURSEMENT_OTHER_VW is a consolidated view of Subjects - Disbursement, SAFI and Student. The MONEY_ANALYTICS_VW contains analytics for funds which are not disbursed.

Views highlighted in RED are top level views.  The column names displayed in the diagram indicate columns used to join to other views.
