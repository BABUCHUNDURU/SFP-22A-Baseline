Student Financial Planning : COD

Created by Stephen Hutchinson, last modified by Angshuman Mukherjee on Feb 18, 2021

The COD Subject contains Common Origination and Disbursement messages sent to and received from Department of Education. The COD_VW is the consolidated view of all origination and disbursement views across all funds. If only a specific fund is of interest then the fund specific view can be used.

Views highlighted in RED are top level views.  The column names displayed in the diagram indicate columns used to join to other views.
