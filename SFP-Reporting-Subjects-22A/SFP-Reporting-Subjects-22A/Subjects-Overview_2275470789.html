Student Financial Planning : Subjects Overview

Created by Buck Foreman, last modified by Angshuman Mukherjee on Nov 16, 2021

These are the defined Subject areas for reporting. Each Subject is composed of one or more views. These views are consolidated from multiple tables that make up the Student Financial Planning application database. Some of these views are related to other views within a subject area or in a different subject area. The details on how these views can be related is found in the detail for each subject area.
