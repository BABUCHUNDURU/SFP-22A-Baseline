Student Financial Planning : Student Examples

Created by Stephen Hutchinson, last modified on Oct 26, 2020

These are examples of queries that reference views in the Student subject.

The code convention $P{INPUT_PARAMETER} is single value input parameter and $X{IN, COLUMN_NAME, INPUT_PARAMETER} is the equivalent of a SQL Language "IN ()" statement. Both of these conventions are used specifically in Jasper Server and map to the input criteria defined within a report definition.

Student Milestone Report
SELECT x1.school_student_id
    , x1.created_datetime
    , x1.sdi_received_date
    , x1.safi_received_date
     , x1.days_to_safi 
     , x1.nslds_received_date
     , x1.days_to_nslds 
     , x1.isir_received_date
     , x1.days_to_isir
     , x1.isir_valid_date  
     , x1.days_to_valid_isir
     , x1.is_course_start_valid   
     , x1.days_to_course_start_valid 
     , x1.is_bbay_start_valid   
     , x1.days_to_bbay_start_valid
     , x1.is_enrollment_status_valid   
     , x1.days_to_enrollment_status_valid
     , x1.is_admission_status_valid   
     , x1.days_to_admission_status_valid  
     , x1.is_academic_status_valid   
     , x1.days_to_academic_status_valid
     , x1.award_date 
     , x1.days_to_award
     , x1.disbursement_date
     , x1.days_to_disburse
     , x1.package_date
     , x1.days_to_package
FROM FASRPT.STUDENT_MILESTONE_VW   x1
WHERE x1.created_datetime BETWEEN  $P{Start_Date} and $P{End_Date}
Student Record Hold Report
SELECT a.school_student_id
     , INITCAP(a.first_name || ' ' || NVL(a.middle_name,'') || ' ' || a.last_name)   as full_name
     , a.held_reason
     , a.datetime_of_action
     , systimestamp - a.datetime_of_action      as days_on_hold
     , a.user_comment
     , a.held_by
FROM FASRPT.STUDENT_RECORD_HOLD_VW  a
WHERE a.held = 'True'
AND   a.held_reason = NVL($P{Student_Record_Hold},a.held_reason)
AND   a.datetime_of_action BETWEEN $P{Start_Date} AND $P{End_Date}
