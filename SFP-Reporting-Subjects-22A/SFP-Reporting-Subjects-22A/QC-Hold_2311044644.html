Student Financial Planning : QC Hold

Created by Stephen Hutchinson, last modified on Oct 26, 2020

The QC Hold Subject contains Student QC Hold Information.  The QC_HOLD_VW object is the consolidated view of all hold types. The other views contain specific hold type data.

Views highlighted in RED are top level views.  The column names displayed in the diagram indicate columns used to join to other views.
