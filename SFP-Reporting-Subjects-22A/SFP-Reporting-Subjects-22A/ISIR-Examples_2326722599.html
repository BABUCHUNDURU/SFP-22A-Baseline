Student Financial Planning : ISIR Examples

Created by Stephen Hutchinson, last modified on Oct 26, 2020

These are examples of queries that reference views in the ISIR subject.

The code convention $P{INPUT_PARAMETER} is single value input parameter and $X{IN, COLUMN_NAME, INPUT_PARAMETER} is the equivalent of a SQL Language "IN ()" statement. Both of these conventions are used specifically in Jasper Server and map to the input criteria defined within a report definition.

ISIR Loading Integration Report
SELECT a.isir_file_name
     , a.file_record_number
     , a.processed_datetime
     , a.error
     , a.record_id
     , a.award_year
     , a.cps_transaction_number
     , a.received_date
     , a.isir_match_status
     , a.student_match_status 
     , a.matched_status
     , a.matched_student_id
     , a.masked_ssn
     , a.first_name
     , a.last_name
     , a.date_of_birth
     , a.matching_student
     , a.isir_status  
     , a.active_isir_for_award_year
     , a.pending_student_documents
     , a.pending_isir_review
     , a.pending_isir_verification
     , a.additional_information
FROM FASRPT.ISIR_LOADING_INTEGRATION_VW  a
WHERE a.processed_datetime BETWEEN  $P{Start_Date} AND  $P{End_Date} 
AND   a.award_year = $P{Award_Year}
